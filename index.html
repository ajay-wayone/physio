WHY MY PHYSIO?

WHAT IS MY PHYSIO?

My Physio is not a clinic.

My Physio is a technology-driven platform that:

  1. Verifies physiotherapists
  2. Classifies them into skill-based levels (L1–L4)
  3. Conducts psychometric screening
  4. Enables geo-based matching
  5. Facilitates secure digital payments
  6. Creates structured earning opportunities

We are building India's most trusted rehabilitation ecosystem.
