The Multi-Layer Verification Engine

The Rehabilitation Infrastructure Model begins with trust.

Every physiotherapist undergoes:

  1. Identity & address verification
  2. Degree & council registration validation
  3. Video verification
  4. Psychometric assessment (ethics, empathy, professionalism)
  5. Agreement to code of conduct

Patients undergo:

  1. Identity verification
  2. Address validation for home visits
  3. Secure digital payment onboarding

This creates a two-sided trust environment.
