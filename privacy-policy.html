Privacy Policy

Your trust is our priority. Learn how we protect your data.

Last Updated: October 2023

Welcome to My Physio. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our platform.

1. Information We Collect

To provide a verified and structured experience, we collect:

  • Personal Identity: Name, contact details, and Government-issued ID for police verification.
  • Professional Data: Qualifications, registration numbers, and experience details for ranking.
  • Financial Information: Bank details or wallet information for transparent earnings and payouts.
  • Usage Data: Performance scores, session analytics, and user interactions.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your information allows us to maintain a high-standard ecosystem:

  • To verify and rank physiotherapists based on merit.
  • To connect patients with verified professionals through our structured technology.
  • To process payments and manage the wallet-based payout system.
  • To provide session completion analytics and professional growth features.

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your professional identity and personal details. Access to sensitive data is restricted to authorized personnel only, ensuring that "professional structure" extends to your data safety.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell your data. We only share information with third parties necessary for:

  • Police and identity verification services.
  • Payment gateway providers for revenue distribution.
  • Regulatory authorities if required by Indian law.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request the deletion of your personal information. However, please note that certain verification data must be maintained to keep your "Verified" status on the platform.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions regarding this policy or your data, please reach out through our contact section or email us at privacy@myphysio.in.
