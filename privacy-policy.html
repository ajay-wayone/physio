Privacy Policy

Your trust is our priority. Learn how we protect your data.

Effective Date: 01.02.2026
Last Updated: 19.03.2026

1. INTRODUCTION

My Physio (“Company”, “We”, “Our”, “Us”) operates a technology platform that connects users (“Patients”) with independent licensed physiotherapists (“Therapists”) for home-based physiotherapy services.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal data in a transparent, secure, and lawful manner.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use the My Physio mobile application (“App”).

By using the App, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

A. Personal Information

  • ● Full name
  • ● Mobile number
  • ● Email address
  • ● Address and geo-location
  • ● Gender
  • ● Date of birth (if required)

B. Health & Medical Information (Sensitive Data)

We may collect the following health-related data:

  • ● Medical condition details
  • ● Symptoms and treatment requirements
  • ● Medical history voluntarily provided by the user
  • ● Therapy preferences
  • ● Session notes recorded by therapists

Purpose of Collection:

This data is collected strictly for:

  • ● Matching patients with appropriate physiotherapists
  • ● Providing physiotherapy treatment services
  • ● Monitoring treatment progress
  • ● Ensuring safety and quality of care

We do NOT use health data for advertising, profiling, or selling to third parties.

C. Therapist Information

  • ● Qualification certificates
  • ● Registration certificates
  • ● Experience details
  • ● Specialization
  • ● KYC documents
  • ● Bank details (for payouts)

D. Payment Information

  • ● Transaction ID
  • ● Payment amount and status
  • ● Refund status

⚠ We do NOT store card or UPI credentials. All payments are processed through RBI-compliant third-party payment gateways.

E. Technical & Usage Data

  • ● Device information
  • ● IP address
  • ● App usage behavior
  • ● Login activity
  • ● Crash reports

F. Communication Data

  • ● In-app chat logs
  • ● Masked call records
  • ● Call recordings (if enabled)

Call recordings are stored for a limited duration (up to 90 days) for safety and dispute resolution purposes only.

3. PURPOSE OF DATA COLLECTION AND USAGE

  • ● Creating and managing user accounts
  • ● Matching patients with suitable physiotherapists
  • ● Enabling booking, scheduling, and session management
  • ● Facilitating communication between users and therapists
  • ● Processing payments and managing escrow transactions
  • ● Providing customer support and resolving disputes
  • ● Ensuring safety, preventing fraud, and enforcing platform policies
  • ● Improving app performance and user experience
  • ● Complying with legal and regulatory requirements

We do NOT sell or rent user data. We do NOT use sensitive health data for advertising.

4. LEGAL BASIS FOR PROCESSING

  • ● User consent
  • ● Contractual necessity (service delivery)
  • ● Legal obligations under applicable Indian laws
  • ● Legitimate interests (fraud prevention, safety, service improvement)
