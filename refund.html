Refund Policy

Transparent payment and refund guidelines for all users of My Physio.

At My Physio, we aim to provide a fair and transparent payment system for both physiotherapists and patients. This Refund Policy outlines the circumstances under which refunds may or may not be granted.

All payments made on the My Physio platform are processed securely. Refunds are handled according to the guidelines mentioned below to maintain fairness across the platform.

1. Registration Fees

The physiotherapist registration fee on the My Physio platform is a one-time onboarding fee.

  • Registration fees are strictly non-refundable.
  • The fee covers verification processes including document checks, psychometric screening, and platform onboarding.
  • Once registration is completed and the verification process begins, refunds cannot be requested.

2. Session Booking Refunds

Refunds for therapy sessions depend on the status of the booking.

  • Before session confirmation: Full refund may be issued.
  • If therapist cancels the session: Full refund will be processed automatically.
  • If patient cancels before the allowed time window: Refund may be issued according to platform rules.
  • If cancellation occurs after the allowed time window: Refund may not be applicable.

3. Failed or Incomplete Sessions

In rare cases where a session cannot be completed due to technical issues or unforeseen circumstances:

  • The session may be rescheduled.
  • Alternatively, a refund or wallet credit may be provided after review.

4. Wallet Credits

Certain refunds may be issued as platform wallet credits instead of direct bank transfers.

  • Wallet credits can be used for future bookings on My Physio.
  • Wallet credits are non-transferable.

5. Processing Time

Once a refund request is approved, the processing timeline is as follows:

  • Wallet refunds: Instant to 24 hours.
  • Bank or payment gateway refunds: 5–10 business days depending on the payment provider.

6. Misuse of Refund Requests

Any misuse of refund requests, fraudulent claims, or repeated disputes may lead to:

  • Suspension of the user account.
  • Permanent removal from the My Physio platform.

7. Policy Updates

My Physio reserves the right to update or modify this Refund Policy at any time to reflect operational, legal, or regulatory requirements.
