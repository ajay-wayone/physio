Refund Policy

Transparent payment and refund guidelines for all users of My Physio.

Effective Date: 24.02.2026
Last Updated: 24.02.2026

1. INTRODUCTION

This Refund & Cancellation Policy governs all cancellations, refunds, disputes, and payment releases made through the My Physio mobile application. By booking a session or package, you agree to this policy.

2. PAYMENT MODEL OVERVIEW

  • ● All payments are collected in advance.
  • ● Payments are held in escrow.
  • ● Funds are released to the therapist after:
    • ● Session completion
    • ● 24-hour complaint window
    • ● No active dispute
  • ● Refund eligibility depends on cancellation timing and circumstances.

3. USER CANCELLATION POLICY

A. Single Session Cancellation

  • ● 24+ hours before session → 100% refund | 0% therapist payment
  • ● 6–24 hours before → 80% refund | 20% therapist payment
  • ● Less than 6 hours → 50% refund | 50% therapist payment
  • ● Therapist reaches location & user cancels → 0% refund | 100% therapist payment

B. Repeated Cancellations

  • ● Future bookings may require strict advance payment
  • ● Refund flexibility may be restricted
  • ● Account may be flagged for misuse

4. PHYSIOTHERAPIST CANCELLATION POLICY

A. Genuine Emergency

  • ● User receives 100% refund
  • ● Therapist receives 0%
  • ● Option to reschedule provided

B. Late Cancellation (< 2 hours)

  • ● 1st occurrence → Warning
  • ● 2nd occurrence → 10% wallet deduction
  • ● 3rd occurrence → 25% penalty
  • ● Repeated → Suspension / Level review
  • ● User always receives 100% refund

C. No Show

  • ● 100% refund to user
  • ● 20% penalty to therapist wallet
  • ● Rating impact applied

5. PACKAGE REFUND POLICY

A. Before Any Session Used

  • ● 0 sessions used → 90% refund

B. Partial Usage

  • ● 1–2 sessions → Pro-rata refund minus 10% admin fee
  • ● 50% used → No refund
  • ● 75%+ used → No refund
  • ● Refunds are calculated on discounted package amount

6. DISPUTE RESOLUTION & PAYMENT HOLD

  • ● Complaints must be raised within 12 hours
  • ● Payment is temporarily frozen
  • ● Internal review is conducted
  • ● Call logs, session notes, and geo data may be reviewed
  • ● Valid complaint → Refund processed
  • ● Invalid complaint → Payment released to therapist
  • ● False complaints may lead to account penalty

7. MISREPRESENTATION OR POLICY VIOLATION

  • ● Fake booking → 40% refund | 60% therapist payment
  • ● Harassment → 0% refund | 100% therapist payment
  • ● Repeated misuse → 0% refund | 100% therapist payment
  • ● Account may be suspended

8. UNSAFE LOCATION SCENARIO

  • ● Therapist may decline service
  • ● 50% payment may be released to therapist
  • ● Remaining 50% refunded to user (subject to review)

9. HOLIDAY & EXTENDED HOUR BOOKINGS

  • ● High-demand slot cancellation → Refund may be limited to 50%
  • ● Therapist cancellation → 100% refund to user
  • ● Additional penalty may apply to therapist

10. REFUND PROCESSING TIME

  • ● 5–10 business days depending on payment gateway
  • ● Delays by banks/payment providers are not controlled by My Physio

11. NON-REFUNDABLE SITUATIONS

  • ● Misuse of SOS
  • ● Harassment or misconduct
  • ● Attempt to bypass platform
  • ● Offline booking attempts

12. FORCE MAJEURE

  • ● Natural disasters
  • ● Government restrictions
  • ● Public emergencies

13. RIGHT TO MODIFY

My Physio reserves the right to update this policy at any time. Updated versions will be reflected within the App.
