ABOUT MY PHYSIO

My Physio is a structured physiotherapy network designed to bring safety, professionalism, and employment into the home healthcare sector.

We connect patients with verified physiotherapists through:

  • Document verification
  • Police compliance
  • Psychometric behavioral assessment
  • Level-Based Skill Classification

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to make physiotherapy easy to access and safe by connecting patients with verified therapists. We help professionals grow and make recovery simple and convenient through digital care.

OUR VISION

To create India's most trusted physiotherapy platform by combining verification, technology, and structured professional growth.

FOUNDER MESSAGE

Healthcare should not be informal, rehabilitation should be structured, professionals deserve recognition and patients deserve safety.

My Physio is built to create both
